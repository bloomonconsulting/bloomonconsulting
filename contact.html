Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the consultation process take?

+

Our initial consultation typically lasts 30-45 minutes. This gives us enough time to understand your business needs and determine how we can help. If we move forward, the timeline for implementation varies based on the complexity of the solutions, but we'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

What types of businesses do you work with?

+

We work primarily with small businesses and clinics. Our expertise spans healthcare practices, therapy centers, professional services, retail businesses, and more. If you're a small business looking to improve efficiency through technology, we'd love to talk to you.

How much does your service cost?

+

Pricing varies based on the scope of work and specific solutions needed. We offer a free initial consultation to discuss your needs and provide a customized quote. Our goal is to provide cost-effective solutions that deliver real value to your business.

Will you train my team on new systems?

+

Absolutely! Training is a crucial part of our service. We provide comprehensive training to ensure you and your team feel comfortable and confident with any new technology we implement. We also offer ongoing support to help troubleshoot issues and optimize your systems.
