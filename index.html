When Your Passion Gets Buried in Paperwork

 

As an expert in your field, your focus should be on your clients. But as you grow, the "business" part starts to take over.

 

The "Manual Treadmill"

You're stuck in a loop of copy-pasting client info from intake forms to calendars to invoices. It's time you're not spending on billable work.

The "Where Is It?" Panic

Client notes are in one doc, contracts are in your email, and session plans are on a sticky note. Nothing is centralized or secure.

The "Growth" Dread

You're so overwhelmed by your current admin that the idea of taking on more clients feels exhausting, not exciting.

 
 
   
 
 

Operations, With a Human Lens

 
 

I'm not a typical tech consultant. I'm a social worker with a decade of nonprofit experience. I know you need solutions that are practical, sustainable, and don't require a team of 10 to manage.

My approach starts with you, not the software. I get to know you, your team, and how you work best. There is no 'one-size-fits-all' solution. My goal is to create a customized, simple system you'll actually use, so you can focus on what's most important: your clients.

 
 
 
 
   
 
 

Our Collaborative Process

 

This is a "done-with-you" process to build a system that lasts.

 

1. We Listen & Assess

We start by getting to know you. We review your current workflows, your tools, and your goals to find the real bottlenecks.

2. We Design Your Custom System

We choose and implement the right tools that fit your style, without overcomplicating things. This is your 'single hub' for everything.

3. We Build & Implement

We build out your new, automated workflows for onboarding, scheduling, and billing so your operations run smoothly and consistently.

4. We Train & Support

A system is useless if it's confusing. We train you and your team until it feels effortless and stay available for support as you grow.
