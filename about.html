Philosophy

Operations, With a Human Lens

My approach to operations comes from a human lens, not a tech manual. I am a social worker with a decade of experience in community-based nonprofits, so I've lived in high-stakes environments where resources are tight. I know that less money means less people, but it doesn't have to mean less efficiency.

At every organization I’ve had the pleasure to be at, I found ways to increase productivity using simple, smart technology. I taught my colleagues these same systems and saw firsthand what happens when people are given the freedom to focus on their real work.

Every organization looks and functions differently. There is no "one-size-fits-all" solution. My goal is to get to know you, your team (if you have one), and how you work best. From there, I will create workflows that actually work for you, make sure you know how to use it, and help you get back to focusing on what's most important: your clients.
