How I Do Human Centered Operations

My name is Malak Baker. I’m an operations and business development consultant helping small business owners and remote service providers modernize outdated systems and run day-to-day operations with less manual work.

I’ll start by making sure I understand you, your business, your working style, and your goals. From there, I build systems that align with how you actually operate and how you learn best. That includes meeting you where you are with technology and creating clear, usable standard operating procedure guides so you can confidently maintain what I put in place with you.

I care a lot about efficiency, but not the performative kind. I focus on the parts of your business that quietly eat time: repetitive admin, scattered tools, unclear handoffs, and processes that rely on memory. I learn platforms quickly and I build workflows that fit the way you naturally work.

I keep your budget in mind when selecting software and tools. I use what you already have whenever possible, and I set up overlooked features that remove extra steps. When new tools are necessary, I choose options that are simple, stable, and worth the cost.

I work with small business owners and remote service providers across service, retail, and community-based work.

Business happens between people. I prioritize real relationships.
