Human-Centered Operations

More about who you'll be working with

My name is Malak Baker. I am an operations and business development consultant helping service providers build businesses that are organized internally and connected externally.

A successful business requires a stable internal foundation and strong external relationships. I address these two deeply connected areas together to create a unified strategy.

I bridge the gap. I help you build the "machine" that runs your business—the software, billing, and workflows—while actively building the network that fuels it through local partnerships and community events.

My background shapes this approach. With over a decade of experience in youth-serving nonprofits and a master’s degree as a licensed macro social worker, I understand systems from a human perspective. I approach operations with an ethic of care, ensuring your business is efficient and sustainable for you as a person.

I work with therapists, coaches, consultants, and creatives. My goal is the same: to give you the structure and connections you need to focus on your craft.
