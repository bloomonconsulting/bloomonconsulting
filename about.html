How I Do Human Centered Operations

Who You'll Be Working With

My name is Malak Baker. I’m an operations and business development consultant helping small business owners and remote service providers modernize outdated systems and run day-to-day operations with less manual work.

We’ll start by making sure I understand you, your business, your working style, and your goals. From there, I build systems that align with how you actually operate and how you learn best. That includes meeting you where you are with technology and creating clear, usable standard operating procedures so you can confidently maintain what we put in place.

I focus on the parts of business that quietly eat time: repetitive admin, scattered tools, unclear handoffs, and workflows that rely on memory. I learn platforms quickly and I build systems that fit the way you naturally work.

I keep your budget in mind when selecting software. I use what you already have whenever possible, and I set up overlooked features that remove extra steps. When a new tool is necessary, I choose options that stay simple, stable, and worth the cost.

I work with service businesses, small teams, and remote-first owners who value structure and relationships.
