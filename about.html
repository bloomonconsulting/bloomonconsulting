How We Do Human Centered Operations

Who You'll Be Working With

My name is Malak Baker. I am an operations and business development consultant helping small business owners and individual service providers build businesses that are organized internally and connected externally.

We work as partners. You provide the expertise on your business and your vision. I synthesize your needs and your unique way of working. Together we find the right technology, partnerships, and processes to support your goals.

I am obsessed with efficiency. I find joy in removing the tedious parts of business ownership through creative technology solutions. I adapt quickly. I rapidly learn new platforms and I commit to understanding exactly how you, your business, and your partnerships function.

I keep your budget in mind when selecting software and tools. A small budget is okay. I have innovative ways of replicating processes for low or no cost. I can write these solutions directly into your systems.

My background shapes this approach. With over a decade of experience in youth-serving nonprofits and a master’s degree as a licensed macro social worker, I understand systems from a human perspective. I approach operations with an ethic of care. This ensures your business is efficient and sustainable for you as a person.

I work with therapists, coaches, consultants, retail businesses, and creatives. My goal is simple. I want to give you the structure and connections you need to focus on your craft.
